ingridients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

    • Steps Needed

    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a large skillet, cook ground beef and sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Simmer for 30 minutes.
    • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
    • Spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer noodles, cheese mixture, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
    • Bake in preheated oven for 30-40 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
