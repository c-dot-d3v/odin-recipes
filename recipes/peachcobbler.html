Peach Cobbler

Description Heading

Below are the ingredients

Ingredients

  • 4 cups sliced fresh peaches
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps Needed

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  • In a bowl, mix sliced peaches with 1/2 cup sugar and set aside.
  • In another bowl, combine flour, remaining sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  • Add milk and melted butter to the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  • Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  • Pour the peaches and their juice over the batter. Do not stir.
  • Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown and bubbly.
