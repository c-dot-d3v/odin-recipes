Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps Needed

  • In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  • Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead until smooth and elastic (about 5-7 minutes).
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  • Preheat oven to 475째F (245째C).
  • Punch down the dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  • Transfer the rolled dough to a greased pizza stone or baking sheet.
  • Spread pizza sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and add your favorite toppings.
  • Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
